In observance of Martin Luther King, Jr. Day, City Hall and all city offices will be closed Monday, January 20, 2020. We will reopen on Tuesday, January 21, 2020, at regular business hours. 

Residential trash pick up will occur as scheduled on Monday, by Morgan Sanitation.
